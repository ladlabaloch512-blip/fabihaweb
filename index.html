Features of PubAd.Media

Unlock powerful tools and smart technologies designed to simplify monetization and maximize your earnings.

Smart Ad Refreshing Technology

Keep your inventory fresh and maximize your ad impressions with intelligent ad refreshing that only triggers when users are actively engaging with your content.

Advanced Smart Tags

Easy-to-implement ad tags that optimize loading speed and ensure smooth ad delivery across all devices and platforms.

PnP (Plug and Play) Integration

Simple and quick setup — no complicated coding required. Start monetizing faster with our plug-and-play solution designed for publishers of all sizes.

Prebid Wrapper for Header Bidding

Access premium demand through advanced header bidding technology. Our Prebid wrapper ensures maximum competition and higher CPMs for your inventory.

Ad Formats

Engaging and high-performing ad units designed for modern publishers.

Display Banners

Classic yet effective, our display banner ads are optimized for viewability and high click-through rates. We support all standard IAB sizes and rich media formats to capture user attention without disrupting the user experience.

Video Ads

Monetize your video content with pre-roll, mid-roll, and out-stream video ads. Our low-latency player and access to premium video demand ensure you get the highest CPMs for your most engaging inventory.

Native Ads

Seamlessly integrate ads that match the look, feel, and function of your content. Native ads provide a non-disruptive user experience, leading to higher engagement and better performance.

Interstitial Ads

Full-screen ads that appear at natural transition points in the user journey, such as between pages or after an action is completed. They command user attention and are perfect for high-impact campaigns.

Push Notification Ads

Reach users even when they are not on your site with browser-based push notifications. This high-engagement format is perfect for delivering timely updates, promotions, and driving users back to your content.
